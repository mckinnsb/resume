It's been a while between updates. I've been working hard on a preview system.

A core element of a lot of strategy games is a little bit of a heads up regarding the result of an action.

I really like the idea of being able to pull complex combos off - like hitting an enemy to your buddy and having him hit the enemy over you - but sometimes it gets to be a little too much to process.

I wanted something that would give a physical representation of the motion in each action. Right now this visual treatment is very temporary, as we are going to overhaul the trap appearance AND the preview.
